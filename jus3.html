ABOUT BRAINTECH
Braintech is an event for young technical minds having knowledge of various engineering streams/ branches. Technical quizzes are a great way to have fun, but they can also be used as a tool for self-improvement. They can help you learn more about yourself and how others can judge you. In a quiz, which is also known as a mind sport, participants compete to answer questions either individually or in teams.
TENTATIVE RULES
Four participants per team (Preferably multidisciplinary branches/trades)
Round 1: (Objective Round)
• Each team would be asked 5 questions. (Objective question)

• 10 marks for correct answer, and no negative marking for wrong answer.

• If a team cannot answer the question, then the question would be forwarded to the next team (NO TIME).

• Answering time is only 30 seconds

Round 2: (Buzzer round)
• This Round would be the buzzer round

• 10 marks for the correct answer and no negative marking for wrong answer

• Total 10 questions will be asked

• Answering time is only 5 seconds
br • This round is not elimination round

Round 3: (Rapid Fire)
• Each team will give 5 questions to answer within the time limit of 50 seconds

• 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5 marks for the wrong or no answer

• Question will be changed after 10 seconds

• 2 teams are to be selected as Winners and Runners
